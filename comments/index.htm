I just played your new game Crisis Wolverine: Insurrection Green from start to finish, and it was the best three hours I have spent in a long time. The game completely sucks you in, making you want to explore the familiar landscape and find the jokes hidden within. Shortness and lack of complexity can be both good and bad, but here only the good seems to come through, leaving a nice, mint-like aftertaste. While playing, I sometimes thought that it was too easy, I mean I only had one party member die in the whole game. However, this ease certainly makes the game much less frustrating, and allows you to focus on the story, and the jokes. The length is also a blessing in disguise, I like Final Fantasy as much as the next red-blooded gamer, but sometimes I don't want to commit 60 or more hours to a game with a streched-to-the-breaking-point stroyline. Anyway, great game, recommending it to all my friends, and planning to donate (really, I just need to borrow someone's paypal account).
