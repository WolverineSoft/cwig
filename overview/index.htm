Game Overview
System: Windows PC
Genre: CW:IG is a top down RPG (ala Final Fantasy and Dragon Warrior) set on the University of Michigan Campus. The player controls a party of up to 4 characters as he/she does battle with Evil MSU forces.
Premise: The player initially controls Trevor, a U of M student, who is stranded on the U of M campus during spring break and uncovers MSU's dastardly plot to overtake it. He must then band with some unlikely partners in an effort to save the U of M and ultimately the world!
Game Play: In CW:IG the player will travel across Central, North, and South Campus in an effort to extinguish the MSU threat. Over the course of the game, the player has to battle MSU forces and solve puzzles in order to progress. The team will find themselves in many likely places (The Union, South Quad, Chem Building) and some unlikely ones (Burton Tower...). Get ready to explore the U of M and defend it.
Humor: Exploration and RPG elements aside, CW:IG is also a humorous take on U of M campus life. We did our best to parody as much of the Michigan experience as possible, and to take lightly some of the more interesting events in Michigan lore. Clearly, we have also taken the opportunity to embrace our school rivalry with MSU. We hope you enjoy this lighthearted, yet compelling RPG.
