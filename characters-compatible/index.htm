Character Profiles

Reason on Campus:
Trevor had been a backup QB for the University of Michigan. When he was a Junior, the Primary QB dropped out for the NFL draft and he was given the chalupa. He was having a perfect season leading up to the Rose Bowl, when in the deciding game versus MSU he recklessly fumbled the football. UofM lost the game and the moment went down in infamy.
He was forced off the team and lost his scholarship.
Play Style:
The Jock is the typical fighter of the group. He has strong melee attacks and can carry the heaviest armor and weapons. He has a very low spell casting ability, if any.
TREVOR
Reason on Campus:
Spring break is the perfect opportunity to use her recreational drugs without having to worry about class. Spring break is also an opportunity to locate drug supplies that would otherwise be off limits.

Play Style:
The Druggie is the White Wizard or the main healer of the group. She will use her extensive drug knowlege to help the team. Her stronger spells have great effect but are accompanied by some negative effects.

SAGE
Reason on Campus:
Due to a late night drinking session before heading off to the tropical Caribbean for Spring Break, she misses her flight and is stranded on campus. She is also without her fellow Sorority sisters.

Play Style:
The Sorority girl is the Black Wizard of the group. She is a powerful mage and the majority of her attacks are offensive.
JORDANA
Reason on Campus:
Spring Break: The perfect time to both get ahead in all of your classes and play all the new video games. With the proper engineering one can sit in front of a computer all day!

Play Style:
The EECS character will be the "Cid" or tech character of the group. He is a utility character with good ranged attacks and disruptive spells.

  
JOHN
Reason on Campus:
The Frat boy is on campus because he has been arrested by the local police. After a car accident the party meets up with him.

Play Style:
The Frat boy is the thief or ninja of the party using his Frisbee skills to assist in the long range attack. He is mainly offensive and attacks with various projectiles.

  
ADAM
Reason on Campus:
Why would anyone ever want to leave? The Music Major loves Ann Arbor and could never think of actually "leaving."

Play Style:
The Music Major is the Bard of the game. She uses spells to lure enemies into traps and put them to sleep. She also is the only character who can hit more than one enemy at a time with her normal attack.
