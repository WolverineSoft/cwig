Controls

**If this game does not appear in correct proportions on your monitor, press the F4 key to make it size correctly.

Controls for Exploring Campus

Arrow Keys: Move your character(s) up, down, left, or right.

Enter: This is the action button. Use it to perform various actions, such as talking to
people, picking up items, opening doors, etc. Just face the person you want to talk
to, or the object you want to perform an action on, and press the action button to
perform the action. Sometimes, you will have to make choices in what to say, or
what to do. When this happens, use the arrow keys to highlight a choice, and then
press the action button to confirm your decision.

Esc: Call up the status menu.

Controls for the Main Menu

Arrow Keys: Move up and down the menu items.

Enter: Confirm/Go to sub menu.

Esc: Cancel/Go to previous menu.

Controls for Battle

Arrow Keys: Move up and down the battle commands.

Enter: Confirm/Use battle command.

Esc: Cancel.

Note: The keys X, C, V, B, and N do the same things as Esc.
