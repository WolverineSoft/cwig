Hints/FAQ

Hints

1. Save often! And save in different save files. Don't Just overwrite one file.

2. Use potions/heal items both during battle and between battles.

3. Take advantage of shops whenever possible. They aren't everywhere and you will need to stock up on items.

4. Use tech skills during battle that improve party defenses and damage enemy defenses. While only attacking may work for some enemies, harder enemies will require tech skill, and item usage to be defeated.

GAMEPLAY RELATED:

1. Stuck outside the Union:

If you are stuck outside the Union (near the cube) and don't know what to do, walk back to the side entrance where you came out of the Union (where the path leads into the Union) and hit enter.

2. Stuck in Bursley:

Below is a detailed walkthrough of Bursley, for your own enjoyment please only read as far as needed to progress in the game

1. Head into Rob's room (the one between the two signs) and talk to him to receive a special item.
2. Go up stairs, head to the right, and get the hammer from behind the monster.
3. Break the boulders, head straight up and get the ocarina from Link.
4. The ocarina is used to travel back and forth through time, use it whenever there is a barrier in your way that you need to pass.
5. Head to the left side of Bursley and you will see two large double doors with stairs behind them. First, go down to the bottom of the screen and search the pot for the boomerang, then head through the doors and up stairs.
6. Walk to the right and talk to the blue tentacle creature, If you are in the past and you have the boomerang the fight begins.
7.After defeating him back up about 4 steps and there should be a put on some red carpet. Get the silver arrow from there.
8. Head right and talk to Gannon, you need to in the present to use the arrow to break his shield.
9. After defeating Gannon go down the stairs behind him and head to the left. When you get to a T-path go up through the double doors (this is where you use the key you received from Gannon)
10. Use the ocarina to remove the barrier. Head to the vase, and use the ocarina again. In the past you will be able to find the superbomb there.
11. Defeat MSU Punk, and head straight down through the double doors and then to your left.
12. Use the Superbomb on John's door. Get the key, rest, and congratulations you've defeated Bursley!

3. Stuck in the music school after the pond:

Right after you beat the pond boss you are teleported to the edge of the pond.
Well there is a path the leads away from the whirlpool to the bottom of the screen, just follow that path and you will be teleported back to N.Campus and you can continue the game.
Sometimes when we play a gain for the 25th time we forget that not everything is obvious, sorry for not adding a little dialog in the school that would tell you where to go.

4. Stuck in Lurie Tower block room:

Walk up to the still blocks and press enter to move them. This is a puzzle so you need to push more than one block to get to the stairs. Exit out the way you came if you want to reset the blocks.
