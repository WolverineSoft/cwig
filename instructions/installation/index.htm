Installation

1.Download the Zip file.

2.Extract the Zip file (you can get WinZip at WinZip.com)

3.Read the file titled "READ THIS FIRST."

4.Click on the Icon with a large Michigan M on it called INSTALL CW-IG

Warning:
Installation of CW-IG takes about 10 minutes.

When you double click on the setup.exe, a small installation initiation screen will pop up and disappear VERY QUICKLY. (IE. It will seem like nothing is happening).

DON'T CLICK ON THE .EXE AGAIN. This will result in an error message.

The window comes in and out so fast that poeple think nothing is happening, but plese just be patient. Please, Don't Click on the .Exe in frustration.

Sit back, relax, and get ready for a kick-ass gaming experience.

5. Follow the on screen instructions to complete installation.

6. Click on the LAUNCH CW-IG short cut and ENJOY.
