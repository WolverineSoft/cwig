Prologue

Spring Break is finally here! It's a time to go on vacation, or to go home and visit family. But not for Trevor, who finds himself stranded on campus.

Trevor is the quarterback for the University of Michigan football team. Well, he was, anyway. He was having a perfect season, until the waning minutes of the game at the Rose Bowl versus MSU, when he made an unprovoked fumble, causing U of M to lose the game. That moment went down in college football infamy. Trevor was forced off the team, and lost his scholarship money.

Now he's stuck on campus for all of Spring Break, but his troubles don't end there. He soon discovers that MSU is invading his campus! Can Trevor stop Michigan State this time? And why are they even here in the first place?
